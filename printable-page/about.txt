====

> Add ability to view your article/static page in printable page mode.

Your printable page will be available at `{{url}}{{php}}echo Config::get('index.slug');{{/php}}/foo?print=1` or `{{url}}{{php}}echo Config::get('index.slug');{{/php}}/foo?print=true` for articles and `{{url}}bar?print=1` or `{{url}}bar?print=true` for pages.

You can create a shortcut link to the printing page by putting this HTML code anywhere in your `article.php` and `page.php` file:

~~~ .html
<a href="?print=1" rel="nofollow">Print</a>
~~~

*[HTML]: Hyper Text Markup Language
